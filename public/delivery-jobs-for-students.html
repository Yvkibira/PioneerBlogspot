How to be a food delivery personnel in Kenya

One student friendly Side hustle to try during this long holiday delivering food for some companies like Uber eats, Glovo, and Jumia foods, and all the only major requirement is having a bike or a relaible means of transporting the goods. This may be the perfect solution for you if you are looking for a flexible and convenient way to earn money as a students.
Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get started:

How to Deliver in Kenya for Uber Eats

Step 1: Download App

Download the Uber Driver App on your phone.

Step 2: Register

To register you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a bicycle
  • Have a smartphone.
  • Use your full legal names as they appear in your identity documents.

Step 3: Select your city

Now that you've registered and signed in, it's time to select your city. Uber Eats is currently available in Nairobi and Mombasa, so select the city where you want to start delivering.

Step 4: Select your vehicle

Select your vehicle:

  • Bicycle | Uber Eats.

    • Requirements:

    • Be at least 18 years old
    • Have a bicycle
    • Valid National ID
    • Police clearance certificate

Step 5: Submit your Documents

Before you can start delivering with Uber Eats, you need to submit your documents for verification. Make sure you meet the document requirements and upload clear images that are readable and show the full document.

Step 6: Document Review

Once you have uploaded your documents, they will be reviewed and approved by the Uber team. Make sure to keep checking the app to see if your documents have been approved. If any of your documents are rejected, you will need to re-upload them. Once all your documents have been approved, your registration process is complete and your account is set up.

Step 7: Insulated bag

To ensure that food stays fresh during delivery, you will need an insulated delivery bag. Uber Eats will provide you with a link to purchase a bag when your account is ready. It's important to note that you should not purchase a bag before receiving communication to do so.

Step 8: Account Activation

Once you've collected your bag, your account will be activated within 24 to 48 hours. You will then be ready to start receiving orders.

For more information visit www.uber.com/ke/en/deliver/

How to Apply for a Delivery Job at Glovo as a Student

Glovo is a popular on-demand delivery service that operates in Kenya and they’re always on the lookout for reliable and motivated individuals to join their team. In this blog post, we’ll walk you through the steps to apply for a delivery job at Glovo as a student.

Step 1: Check the requirements

Before you apply, it’s important to make sure you meet the minimum requirements for the job. To be a delivery driver for Glovo, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a valid driver’s license
  • Own a scooter, motorcycle, car, or bicycle (depending on the city)
  • Have a smartphone with internet access
  • Be able to speak the local language fluently

If you meet all of these requirements, you’re ready to move on to the next step.

Step 2: Download the app and create an account

To apply for a delivery job at Glovo, you’ll need to download the Glovo app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Once you’ve downloaded the app, you can create an account by providing your email address and creating a password.

Step 3: Submit your application

Once you’ve created an account, you’ll need to submit your application to become a delivery driver. In the app, navigate to the “Become a courier” section and fill out the application form. This will typically include basic information about yourself, such as your name, address, and contact information, as well as details about the vehicle you’ll be using for deliveries.

Step 4: Attend an onboarding session

If your application is successful, you’ll be invited to attend an onboarding session. This is where you’ll receive training on how to use the Glovo app, how to pick up and deliver orders, and how to provide excellent customer service. You’ll also be given a company-branded delivery bag and any other necessary equipment.

Step 5: Start delivering!

Once you’ve completed your onboarding session, you’ll be ready to start delivering orders for Glovo. Simply log into the app, accept orders that fit your schedule, and start delivering. You’ll be paid for each completed delivery, and you can choose to work as much or as little as you like.

For more information visit https://couriers.glovoapp.com/ke/

How to Deliver in Kenya for Jumia Delivery

Step 1: Requirements

Before applying to become a delivery agent for Jumia Delivery in Kenya, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • You must be at least 18 years of age.
  • You must have a valid Kenyan National ID.
  • You must have a smartphone that is compatible with the Jumia Delivery app.
  • You must have a reliable means of transportation (motorbike or bicycle).
  • You must have a clean driving record (if applicable).

Step 2: Download the Jumia Delivery App

To deliver for Jumia Delivery in Kenya, you will need to download the Jumia Delivery app from the Google Play Store or App Store. Once downloaded, you can create an account on the app and start the application process.

Step 3: Apply to Become a Delivery Agent

To apply to become a delivery agent for Jumia Delivery in Kenya, you will need to complete an online application form that is available on the Jumia Delivery app. The application form will require you to provide personal details such as your name, contact information, and means of transportation. You will also need to upload scanned copies of your National ID and driving license (if applicable).

Step 4: Attend an Interview and Training

After submitting your application, you will be contacted by Jumia Delivery for an interview and training session. During the interview, you will be asked questions about your personal details, driving experience, and motivation for becoming a delivery agent. If successful, you will be invited for a training session where you will be taught how to use the Jumia Delivery app and how to deliver items to customers safely and efficiently.

Step 5: Start Delivering

Once you have completed the training, you will be ready to start delivering for Jumia Delivery in Kenya. To start delivering, you will need to log in to the Jumia Delivery app and select the available delivery orders that fit your schedule. You will then need to collect the items from the designated pick-up point and deliver them to the customers within the designated time frame.

Remember to always follow the guidelines and instructions provided to ensure safe and efficient delivery services.
